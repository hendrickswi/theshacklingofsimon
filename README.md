Here lies a Binding of Isaac ripoff being made for a school project. Kill enemies by shooting them with projectiles and explore the rooms to gain items to grow stronger and kill the boss. (WIP)<br>

Controls (Keyboard):<br>
WASD for directional movement<br>
Arrow keys for shooting<br>
LShift/RShift/E for bombs<br>
SPACE to use the equipped active item<br>
esc to pause the game<br>


Temporary controls for sprint 4:<br>
I to equip the next active item<br>
U to equip the previous active item<br>
J to equip the next primary weapon<br>
K to equip the next secondary weapon<br>
R to reset player<br><br>


Controls (Gamepad):<br>
Left stick for directional movement<br>
Right stick for directional shooting<br>
B/Left Shoulder/Left Trigger for bombs<br>
A/Right Shoulder/Right Trigger to use the equipped active item<br>
START (or + on Nintendo controllers) to pause the game<br>

Temporary controls for sprint 4:<br>
D-Pad UP to equip the next active item<br>
D-Pad DOWN to equip the previous active item<br>
D-Pad LEFT to equip the next primary weapon<br>
D-Pad RIGHT to equip the next secondary weapon<br>
BACK (or - on Nintendo controllers) to reset player<br><br>


Temporary mouse controls:<br>
Left click to move "foward" one room<br>
Right click to move "backward" one room<br><br>


KNOWN ISSUES:<br>
Player head sprite is slightly off center sometimes<br>
There is jittering when any dynamic entity continuously moves into a blocking tile<br><br>
